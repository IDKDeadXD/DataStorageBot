How It Works: Simple & Secure

1

Store Your Data

Use commands like /storetext or /storeattachments. Provide a name for your item and the content/file.

2

Instant Encryption

Your data is immediately encrypted with your unique user-derived key before being sent to the private storage channels on Discord.

3

Retrieve Securely

Use /retrievedata. Select your item from a dropdown. It's decrypted and shown only to you (ephemerally).
